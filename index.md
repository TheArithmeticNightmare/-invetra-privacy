## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide and improve the App and to comply with legal obligations.

### 1.1 Account and authentication

- **Email address** — when you sign in with email or request a sign-in link.
- **Name and profile data** — if provided by your Google or Microsoft account when you sign in with those providers.
- **Account identifiers** — such as user IDs created by our authentication provider (Firebase / Google).

You may also use the App as a guest (without creating an account). In that case we do not collect account identifiers linked to you personally, but usage and inventory data may still be stored on your device and, if you use backup or collaboration features, on our or third-party systems.

### 1.2 Inventory and business data

- **Stocktake sessions** — session names, items, counts, notes, variance data, and related information you enter in the App.
- **Collaboration data** — session codes and participation in shared stocktake sessions, if you use that feature.
- **Activity and backup logs** — records of actions such as backups, restores, and imports (e.g. for troubleshooting and audit).

This data is primarily business/operational data. Where it is linked to your account or device, we treat it in line with this policy.

### 1.3 Device and technical information

- **Device type and operating system** — e.g. Android version, device model, to support compatibility and troubleshooting.
- **App usage** — such as feature usage and error logs, to improve stability and performance.

We do not use this information to track you across other apps or websites for advertising.
